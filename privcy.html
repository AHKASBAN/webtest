Privacy Policy

QuadraBay collects information required to conduct its business functions and services. QuadraBay recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of anyone submitting their data to us. Any information shared between QuadraBay and its applicants, site users or clients is protected in accordance with applicable laws and the policies stated below.

QuadraBay collects full name, address, email address, telephone and fax numbers and other directory information as provided by applicants or clients. Personal information is used only in accordance with service provision.

QuadraBay keeps information on file in accordance with regulations governing our industry, for the purpose of future requests for services, credential verification and/or compliance with requests from legal and Government authorities.

Applicants may receive contact from us on the phone numbers or email addresses they provided to us. Applicants who do not wish to receive such contact should advise QuadraBay per the contact details listed on this site.

QuadraBay does not release information to third parties except as follows:

  • To authorized officers of institutions or agencies that an applicant has designated as a recipient for his/her report;
  • To official authorities in cases of confirmed fraudulent or forged documents;
  • To comply with a judicial order or a lawfully issued subpoena.
